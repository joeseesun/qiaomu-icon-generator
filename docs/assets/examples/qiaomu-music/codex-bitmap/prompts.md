# Qiaomu Music Icon Options - Prompt Set

Shared constraints for all options:
- 1024x1024 square master app icon composition, opaque background, no transparency.
- Must work for iOS app icon and website favicon: bold silhouette, centered, readable at 32px.
- No text, no letters, no numbers, no app screenshots, no watermark, no brand/logo copying.
- Keep clear padding; avoid thin lines and tiny details.
- Visual identity: Qiaomu Music, dark refined music player, current palette anchors #0f0f0f and #ffbe3b, with optional #49c9ff and #ff4d7d accents.
- Inspiration: curated iOS music icons from local Icon Museum references, especially clean layered album/playlist geometry, retro cassette/vinyl forms, professional audio controls, and warm modern gradients.

Options:

1. Vinyl Sun: amber vinyl-disc sun on deep black, subtle grooves, small abstract play cutout.
2. Echo Rings: concentric audio rings as a tree-ring/vinyl hybrid with amber core and cyan arcs.
3. Modern Cassette: simplified retro cassette window and reels, no label, amber central glow.
4. Wave Leaves: thick waveform bars rising like abstract leaves around an amber circle.
5. Abstract Q Disc: current-favicon evolution, amber disc plus tonearm/notch hinting at Q without literal text.
6. Album Stack: layered playlist/album tiles with a record edge and front waveform groove.
7. Night Radio: circular tuning dial and broad broadcast arcs for a late-night listening mood.
8. Liquid Wave: thick cyan/magenta waveform ribbon flowing through a small amber record core.
9. Pocket Turntable: simplified premium top-down turntable with amber platter and tonearm.
10. Sound Pearl: glowing amber orb carved by three waveform grooves.
